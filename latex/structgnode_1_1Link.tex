\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a connection between two nodes in a graph. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{structgnode_1_1Link}{Link}} struct holds the details of a connection between two nodes, including the identifiers of the nodes and the ports involved in the connection. This information is used to define and manage the connections within a graph.
